<section class="lexique-letter" id="a">
<h2>A</h2>
<dl>
<div class="lexique-term">
<dt>Arbitrage</dt>
<dd>Transferring all or part of the invested sums from one underlying to another within the same life insurance contract or PEA.</dd>
</div>
</dl>
</section>

<section class="lexique-letter" id="c">
<h2>C</h2>
<dl>
<div class="lexique-term">
<dt>Capitalization</dt>
<dd>Mechanism by which the interest generated by an investment is reinvested to generate additional interest in turn.</dd>
</div>
</dl>
</section>

<section class="lexique-letter" id="d">
<h2>D</h2>
<dl>
<div class="lexique-term">
<dt>Defiscalization (tax relief)</dt>
<dd>All legal mechanisms allowing a reduction of income tax or real estate wealth tax.</dd>
</div>
</dl>
</section>

<section class="lexique-letter" id="l">
<h2>L</h2>
<dl>
<div class="lexique-term">
<dt>Life insurance</dt>
<dd>Long-term savings product allowing capital to be built with favourable taxation after 8 years, optimised transmission and a wide choice of underlyings.</dd>
</div>
<div class="lexique-term">
<dt>Livret A</dt>
<dd>French regulated savings passbook, tax-free, capped at 22,950 euros for an individual.</dd>
</div>
</dl>
</section>

<section class="lexique-letter" id="p">
<h2>P</h2>
<dl>
<div class="lexique-term">
<dt>PEA (Equity Savings Plan)</dt>
<dd>Tax wrapper allowing investment in European equities with exemption from income tax on gains after 5 years.</dd>
</div>
<div class="lexique-term">
<dt>PER (Retirement Savings Plan)</dt>
<dd>Long-term savings product deductible from income tax at entry, taxed at exit.</dd>
</div>
<div class="lexique-term">
<dt>Private Equity</dt>
<dd>Investment in the capital of unlisted companies, usually with a holding horizon greater than 5 years.</dd>
</div>
</dl>
</section>

<section class="lexique-letter" id="r">
<h2>R</h2>
<dl>
<div class="lexique-term">
<dt>Return (Yield)</dt>
<dd>Performance of an investment expressed as a percentage, calculated on an annual basis.</dd>
</div>
</dl>
</section>

<section class="lexique-letter" id="t">
<h2>T</h2>
<dl>
<div class="lexique-term">
<dt>APR (TAEG)</dt>
<dd>Annual Percentage Rate. Includes all the costs of a loan (interest, insurance, fees). Main indicator for comparing credit offers.</dd>
</div>
</dl>
</section>

<section class="lexique-letter" id="u">
<h2>U</h2>
<dl>
<div class="lexique-term">
<dt>Unit-linked funds</dt>
<dd>Investment supports in life insurance whose value fluctuates according to financial markets (equities, bonds, real estate).</dd>
</div>
</dl>
</section>

<section class="lexique-letter" id="w">
<h2>W</h2>
<dl>
<div class="lexique-term">
<dt>Wealth tax (IFI)</dt>
<dd>French real estate wealth tax. Applies to net real estate assets above 1.3 million euros.</dd>
</div>
</dl>
</section>
