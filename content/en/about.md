Quel Placement is an independent blog dedicated to personal finance. Our mission: make investing accessible to everyone, without jargon or sales pitch.

## Our editorial line

We publish factual analyses across six main topics:

- **Taxation**: understand the finance law, income tax, wealth tax, tax-efficient schemes
- **Private Equity**: invest in unlisted assets (FCPR, FIP, FCPI)
- **Savings**: Livret A, LDDS, life insurance, PEA, PER
- **Credit**: real estate, consumer, refinancing
- **Bank Account**: comparisons of online banks, neobanks, traditional banks
- **Insurance**: auto, home, protection, health

## Our commitment

- Content reviewed by industry professionals
- No sponsored placement without explicit disclosure
- Regular updates based on tax and financial news
- Sources cited for every figure
