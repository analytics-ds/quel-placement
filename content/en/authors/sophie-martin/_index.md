## About Sophie

Sophie Martin has over 10 years of experience in independent financial journalism. Trained in market finance, she collaborated with several specialised media before joining Quel Placement as lead writer. Her mission: make wealth topics accessible to everyone, without jargon or commercial bias.

## Her areas of expertise

- **Savings and investment**: Livret A, LDDS, LEP, life insurance, PEA
- **Retirement**: PER, annuity, capital, tax optimisation
- **Private Equity**: FCPR, FIP, FCPI, holding company
- **Taxation**: income tax, IFI, tax loopholes, Jeanbrun scheme, capital gains
- **Mortgage**: rates, APR, usury rate, refinancing, renegotiation
- **Insurance**: borrower (Lemoine law), protection, complementary health

## Her editorial approach

Every article rests on three pillars:

1. **Factuality**: official sources (Banque de France, DGFiP, Finance Law), verified figures, no sponsored placement without disclosure
2. **Pedagogy**: short definitions, comparison tables, figured practical cases
3. **Neutrality**: no influence from a specific institution or product

Her goal: help every reader make informed financial decisions by understanding tax mechanisms and wealth arbitrages.
